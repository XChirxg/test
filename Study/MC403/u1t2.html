Level at Which Strategy Operates

  • Strategies operate at various levels within an organization, each with its own scope and focus.
  • Understanding these levels helps in aligning strategies with organizational goals and objectives.

Corporate Level Strategy

  • Concerned with the overall scope and direction of the organization.
  • Involves decisions related to the company's mission, vision, and long-term objectives.
  • Determines the allocation of resources across different business units and geographical regions.

Business Unit Level Strategy

  • Focused on individual business units or divisions within the organization.
  • Addresses competitive dynamics within specific industries or markets.
  • Involves decisions related to market positioning, product development, and competitive advantage.

Functional Level Strategy

  • Concerned with the activities of individual functional areas such as marketing, finance, operations, etc.
  • Supports the achievement of business unit and corporate-level objectives.
  • Includes decisions related to resource allocation, process optimization, and functional integration.
