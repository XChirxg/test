Strategic Management - Meaning and Process

  • Strategic management is the process of formulating and implementing strategies to achieve organizational goals.
  • It involves analyzing internal and external factors, setting objectives, and developing strategies.
  • The process includes strategy formulation, implementation, and evaluation to ensure the organization's long-term success.

Importance of Strategic Management

  • Helps organizations set clear goals and objectives.
  • Ensures efficient allocation of resources.
  • Adapts to changing market conditions.

Key Components of Strategic Management

  • Strategic planning: Setting goals and objectives.
  • Implementation: Executing strategies.
  • Evaluation: Assessing strategy effectiveness.

Strategic Management Process

  • Environmental scanning: Analyzing internal and external factors.
  • Strategy formulation: Developing strategies based on analysis.
  • Strategy implementation: Executing strategies.
  • Strategy evaluation: Assessing strategy effectiveness.
