Strategic Management: Awareness and Change

  • Awareness of strategic management involves understanding the importance of strategic planning and its impact on organizational success.
  • Change is a constant in strategic management, as organizations must adapt to new market conditions, technologies, and competitive pressures.
  • Strategic management helps organizations anticipate change, respond effectively, and stay competitive in dynamic environments.

Importance of Strategic Awareness

  • Enhances organizational performance and competitiveness.
  • Improves decision-making and resource allocation.
  • Increases adaptability to changing market conditions.

Managing Change in Strategic Management

  • Recognizing the need for change based on environmental scans and performance evaluations.
  • Implementing change through strategic initiatives and organizational restructuring.
  • Evaluating the effectiveness of change efforts and making adjustments as needed.

Strategic Change Models

  • Lewin's Change Management Model: Unfreeze, Change, Refreeze.
  • Kotter's 8-Step Change Model: Create a Sense of Urgency, Form a Powerful Coalition, Create a Vision for Change, Communicate the Vision, Remove Obstacles, Create Short-Term Wins, Build on the Change, Anchor the Changes in Corporate Culture.
