Organizational Mission, Vision, and Objectives

  • The organizational mission is a statement that defines the purpose and reason for the existence of the organization.
  • The vision is a statement that describes the desired future state or long-term goal of the organization.
  • Objectives are specific, measurable targets that help the organization achieve its mission and vision.

Organizational Mission

  • Defines the organization's core purpose and reason for existence.
  • Provides a clear direction for the organization's activities and decision-making.
  • Should be concise, meaningful, and inspiring.

Organizational Vision

  • Describes the desired future state or long-term goal of the organization.
  • Inspires and motivates employees and stakeholders.
  • Should be ambitious yet achievable.

Organizational Objectives

  • Specific, measurable targets that support the organization's mission and vision.
  • Help define the organization's priorities and guide decision-making.
  • Should be SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound.
