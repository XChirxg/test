Types of Entrepreneurs

Small Business Entrepreneurs

Small business entrepreneurs are individuals who start and operate small-scale businesses. They are often involved in traditional sectors and focus on meeting local market needs.

Scalable Start-up Entrepreneurs

Scalable start-up entrepreneurs are individuals who pursue high-growth ventures with the potential for rapid expansion and scalability. They often seek investment to fuel growth and aim to capture larger market opportunities.

Social Entrepreneurs

Social entrepreneurs are individuals who address social or environmental issues through innovative business models. They prioritize social impact over profit and seek sustainable solutions to societal challenges.
