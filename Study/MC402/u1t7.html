Types of Startups

Technology Startups

Technology startups focus on developing innovative technologies or leveraging existing technologies to create new products or services. They often operate in fast-paced industries such as software, hardware, and biotechnology.

Social Impact Startups

Social impact startups are driven by a mission to create positive social or environmental change. They address pressing issues such as poverty, healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability through innovative business models.

Lifestyle Startups

Lifestyle startups cater to consumers' lifestyle preferences and needs. They focus on products or services that enhance people's quality of life, such as health and wellness, fashion, food, and travel.

Small Business Startups

Small business startups are typically traditional businesses that provide goods or services to a local market. They often focus on meeting the specific needs of their community and may include retail stores, restaurants, and service providers.

High-Growth Startups

High-growth startups, also known as "gazelles," are businesses that experience rapid growth in a short period. They often attract significant investment and aim to capture a large market share quickly.
